# Should public high schools start later in the morning?

Many districts open between 7:30 and 8:00 a.m. Some education
researchers argue for later start times (8:30 or later). How strongly
do you support shifting public high school start times to **8:30 a.m.
or later**?

Click anywhere on the bar to set your answer. There is no thumb yet —
that is intentional, so the position you choose is your own.

---

- 0: Strongly oppose
- 50: Neither support nor oppose
- 100: Strongly support
